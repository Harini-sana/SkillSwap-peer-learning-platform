Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a skill exchange?

Simply sign up, add the skills you can teach and want to learn, then browse or use the AI suggestions to match!

Is SkillSwap free to use?

Yes, the core platform is free! You can upgrade to Premium for verified badges or featured listings.

Can I teach multiple skills?

Absolutely! You can list as many teaching or learning skills as you like.
